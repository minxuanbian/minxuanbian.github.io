10,000 Moments

This work focuses on: Fleeting moments of life during the pandemic

This work began from: Isolation, digital existence, and the search for meaning within stillness

The work asks: Can small, almost invisible moments sustain human connection in times of distance?

Form / Method: Network-based devised performance, large-scale collaboration

Year: 2022

Location: Nationwide, China

Duration: Pandemic period
