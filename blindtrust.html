Blind Trust

Black box performance

This work explores the limits of collaboration.

It begins with a situation where one must trust without certainty — not only others, but also oneself.

The process challenges the belief in one’s own perception, and questions how far that trust can be sustained when shared with others.

The work asks:

How deeply can we trust ourselves and those around us?
What happens when we risk everything — even an entire stage — on that trust?
