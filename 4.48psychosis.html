4.48 Psychosis

This work focuses on: Dislocation between memory and reality.

This work began from: A moment where memory overlapped with dream — yet sound felt compressed and distorted.

The work asks: When memory fractures, do you trust memory, or yourself?

Form / Method: Displacement

Year: 2025

Location: Shanghai, China

Duration: November 2025
