Reincarnation

This work focuses on: Cycles of seasons and personal choices within reincarnation

This work began from: Observations of seasonal change and philosophical imagination of rebirth

The work asks: After revisiting and forgetting the past, will you stay or move forward?

Form / Method: Cyclical structure

Year: 2024

Location: Nanjing, China

Duration: Jan – May 2024
