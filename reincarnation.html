Reincarnation

2024

A cyclical structure exploring seasonal return, memory, and personal choice.

This work focuses on cycles within the changing seasons — how repetition and return shape individual decisions, and how one confronts what remains unresolved.

It began from observations of natural transitions, combined with an imagined philosophical framework of reincarnation and continuity.

Through repeated revisiting of past moments — remembered and forgotten — the work creates a space where time folds back onto itself.

The work asks:

After continuous return and re-experiencing — what will you choose?
To remain within repetition, or to step forward?

Form / Method: Cyclical structure

Year: 2024
Location: Nanjing, China
Duration: Jan 2024 – May 2024
