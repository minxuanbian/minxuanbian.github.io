Minxuan Bian

Minxuan Bian is a theatre director working between perception, memory, and relational space.

Her work focuses on unstable temporalities — how time folds, dissolves, and reappears through the body.

She is particularly interested in moments where reality becomes uncertain: memory loss, misrecognition, and fragile trust.

Rather than constructing fixed narratives, her directing practice builds situations rather than stories.

Her recent works explore Alzheimer's disease, collaborative limits, and fragile agency within collective structures.
