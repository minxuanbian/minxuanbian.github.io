The Gravity

Project in Development

The gravity in people’s words and actions.

Individuals within a democracy may hold the power to change the world. But how can they carry this power within themselves?

How do they face the community around them — how do they lead, and how do they confront failure?

Democracy encourages individuals to speak, but also asks them to be responsible for what they bring into the world.

If it ends in success, that gravity becomes a force that attracts others. But before that — one must first bet on oneself.
